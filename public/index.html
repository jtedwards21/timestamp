Usage: Pass a unix timestamp or a natural date

Example Usage:

https://timestamp78.herokuapp.com/December 17, 2016 https://timestamp78.herokuapp.com/1482019200000

Output: Returns Natural Date and Unix Time in JSON format

Example Output:

{"natural":"December 17, 2016","unix":1481932800000} {"natural":"December 18, 2016","unix":"1482019200000"}
